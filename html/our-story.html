Founded in 2018 by Patrick Kooky, our mission is to bring you the highest quality yet unusual cookies. Since our start we have already produced dozens of surprising recipes that has shaken the baking community. Our featured Salmon Cookies has been reknown for its eat-a-bility as a dessert and meal. With its high protein content, it will satisfy every craving you had!

At Pat's Salmon Company, we believe in creating gourmet cookies with only the finest ingredients. We have partnered with local farmers and fisheries that obtain fresh yet sustainable ingredients just for you. So you know, when you take a bite of our Salmon Cookies you are really tasting the Pacific Northwest.
